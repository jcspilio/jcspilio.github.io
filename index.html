> hello, world!

I’m a graduate student in the Department of Economics and Oriel College, University of Oxford. My research and teaching span quantitative methods and their applications to health care, law, and the social sciences.

I’m also the president of Economists Without Borders Oxford and a One Young World Ambassador. Before that, I was a College Lecturer in Mathematics at Oriel, and held positions at Stanford, Worcester College, LSE, the University of Sydney, Université Paris-Dauphine and the Parliament of Monaco. Back in simpler times, I served as Arts Editor for La Péniche, Sciences Po’s student newspaper.
